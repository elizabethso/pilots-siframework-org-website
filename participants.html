Criteria for Participating in Pilots

Each S&I pilot should include multiple care settings tied together by a real-world health information exchange use case. Pilots should attempt to include all the initiative pilot goals, however, the pilot may concentrate on one or more goals that may be applicable. Pilots must include participation and support from clinicians, settings of care and the health information technology vendors (e.g. EHR, LIS, HIE, HISP).

Participant Benefits

  • Ability to leverage initiative resources
  • Ensure higher level of compliance
  • Contribute to the community
  • Be recognized as an early adopter

How to Get Involved

To participate as a pilot participant:

  1. Visit the S&I Framework Wiki to learn more about the several initiative Pilots Workgroups
  2. Register as a committed member on the wiki for the interested initiative
  3. Email the ONC S&I Framework team members (admin@siframework.org) to get further guidance
  4. Propose a specific pilot, describing stakeholders, provider types, goals and measurable pilot objectives that provide feedback
  5. Commit adequate resources to successfully complete the proposed scope
