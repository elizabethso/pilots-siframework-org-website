Kolusu, Ma'rage

My Practice in Internal Medicine requires that I consult subspecialties for medical opinions and treatment for different conditions. By partnering with HITivity organization as a HISP we will be able to send the consultation request from the encounter note of the patient within the EMR, PeakPractice by Allscripts to a specialist capable of consuming the transformed CDA document conformant to the S&I framework ToC document for Clinical Summary. This will expedite the care of the patient and the sub specialist will have all the core and pertinent data of the patient. After the consultant sees the patient, he/she can send back his recommendations/plan back to our EMR through HITivity. The plan of the consultant will then be shared with the patient by sending it to the patient's health record at Marâ€™age PHR solution. We believe in acting as a patient advocate and also guide and direct the patient to literature pertinent to their condition. As the era of patient empowerment is taking root and government is advocating patient and their family involvement, education and partnership in their own healthcare, sending the plan and recommendations of the sub specialist will greatly help the patient in terms of less anxiety, better understanding/compliance.
