ToC Pilot XYZ

Enable Healthcare, MEDfx and Techsant Technologies are partnering to pilot and demonstrate transition of care using the ToC Reference Implementation (RI).

This pilot objective is to demonstrate the following ToC services:

  • Validation
  • Transformation
  • ToC Information Packets
  • Vocabulary Mapping
  • Provider Directories [optional]

Participant Benefits

  • Enhance automated interoperability; provider-to-provider and provider-to-patient
  • Assure that high priority health care data can flow rapidly, accurately, and completely
  • Lower health care costs by increasing worker productivity and improving health status
  • Encourage the adoption of internationally recognized data exchange standards
  • Encourage cooperation between multiple vendors
  • Demonstrate that ambitious Meaningful Use Phase 2 goals are reasonable
  • Get a head start on Meaningful Use Phase 2 implementation for earlier certification and market advantage
  • Allow improvements in the quality of clinical information thatâ€™s being transferred through the ability to measure and enforce, using automated validation testing, and adherence to data standards
  • Enhance patient continuity of care, both temporally and geographically
  • Accomplish successful transition of care between diverse health care settings and environments
  • Allow secure automated health data transfer between providers and their patients
  • Reduce the time and costs required for creation of new minimal unstructured summaries and structured summaries
  • Enhance patient clinical outcomes
  • Provide easy transformations of data from machine-readable to user-friendly human-readable formats
  • Allow patients and families to more readily have self-awareness and involvement in their healthcare
  • Facilitate new initiatives: (1) Virtual Lifetime Electronic Record (VLER), (2) Accountable Care Organizations, (3) Patient Centered Medical Homes, and (4) Bundled Payment Models
  • Leverage existing interoperability standards: (1) HITSP specifications, (2) Health Level Seven (HL7) Continuity of Care Documents (CCD), (3) ASTM Continuity of Care Records (CCR)
  • Support Evidenced Based Medicine research initiatives such as Comparative Effectiveness Research (CER)

How to Get Started

To get started as a prospective pilot participant:

  1. Visit the ToC Demos & Pilots Wiki (http://wiki.siframework.org/ToC+Demos+%26+Pilots) to learn more about Pilots Workgroup activities
  2. Participate in the ToC Pilots Workgroup and attend the weekly WG call
  3. Call or email the ONC S&I Frameowork team members (Ann Clarke, Ann.R.Clarke@lmco.com) to get further guidance

How to Join

To join this pilot team:

  1. Visit the ToC Demos & Pilots Wiki (http://wiki.siframework.org/ToC+Demos+%26+Pilots) to learn more about Pilots Workgroup activities (Check this pilot charter)
  2. Register as a committed member on the ToC Demos & Pilots Wiki
  3. Call or email the Pilot Team lead expressing your interest and to get further guidance
  4. Propose your contribution to this pilot, objectives and expectations
  5. Commit adequate resources to successfully complete the proposed scope
